# Product

## Register

product

## Users
General users and gamers wanting to build a custom PC. They are looking for an intuitive way to assemble components without worrying about compatibility issues.

## Product Purpose
A smart PC builder application that guides users in selecting fully compatible computer hardware, estimating prices, and providing intelligent presets (gaming, animation, budget). Success means users feel confident in their custom build choices.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Tech-savvy, Reliable. The interface should feel cutting-edge but trustworthy.

## Anti-references
- Not a generic SaaS dashboard
- Not a boring gray form

## Design Principles
- Expert confidence: guide the user clearly, showing we know hardware compatibility.
- Show, don't tell: visualize the PC build progress rather than just listing text.
- Tech-savvy clarity: keep it modern without sacrificing readability and ease of use.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Standard WCAG AA contrast for text readability.
- Clear visual cues for hardware conflicts (not just color-dependent).
